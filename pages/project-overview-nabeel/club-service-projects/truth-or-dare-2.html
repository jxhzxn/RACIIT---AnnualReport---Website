Truth Or Dare 2.0

Halloween is a holiday celebrated each year on October 31, while the origin of this festival is traced back into many years, it is now one the most popular, and awaited days of the year. However, owing to the pandemic crisis in the country, parties and celebrations were annulled, leaving all the Halloween enthusiasts rather discouraged.

Owing to the good feedback received following the first edition of Truth or Dare, the Rotaract club of IIT decided to hold the second edition, but with a little change. To make it more eventful than the last time, we thought of having a Halloween based truth or dare which was more suitable as Halloween was right around the corner.

Truth or Dare 2.0 ,was held on both the 31st of October and 1st of November and was carried out on Google Meets. All the registered participants were asked to participate in teams of 4 or 5 members and were requested to complete 4 rounds of truth or dare which included one Halloween based round. Additionally, to spice up this event, participants were asked to dress up for Halloween to compliment the theme, and bonus points were given for the effort put in. This time, Truth or Dare was open to anyone and everyone, including RACIITians.

This Club service initiative which was organized for the sole purpose of promoting fellowship and membership within the members of our club and other clubs, met its project objective as we reached out to over 80+ Rotaractors and non-rotaractors, who left a great deal of positive comments regarding the enlightenment the project brought especially in times such as these. After two days of fun filled laughter and excitement, we were able to conclude the competition with the announcement of the winners who were rewarded.
