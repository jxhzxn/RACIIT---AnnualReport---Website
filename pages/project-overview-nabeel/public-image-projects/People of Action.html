People of Action

“You have not lived today until you have done something for someone who can never repay you.”- John Bunyan

Helping others without expecting anything in return is a selfless act. The joy of giving arises from a generous heart and sometimes, nothing can replace the joy of helping someone. One may only come to know of that joy by giving without retaining any holdbacks.

People of Action is a campaign brought forward by Rotary, to showcase what happens when Rotarians and leaders from various clubs within the movement come together, share their visions, exchange their ideas, and take necessary action to fulfill these hopes and dreams.

Likewise, the Rotaract Club of IIT, established under the Rotary club of Pan Colombo in 2010 contributed towards this initiative by showcasing a glimpse of our support towards the community. This campaign is mainly focused on those out there, who do not know us and what we do. As Rotaractors or Rotarians we work together in our own separate communities to inspire, transform, connect, and celebrate as one. Additionally, we also know how to work with one another and through this campaign we aim to motivate, inspire, and engage current and prospective members, supporters, partners and volunteers.
