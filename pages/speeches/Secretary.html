Message from the Secretary

Secretary

Rtr. Amesha Vithanage

“Uncertain as the situation seemed, tough as the days had been, unusual it was for RACIIT.”

However, it is with absolute pride and gratitude that I pen a few words as the secretary of the Rotaract Club of IIT, 2020-21.

In an era of “when will things ever go back to normal?”, Rotaract Club of IIT was able to reach numerous milestones in terms of escalating the count of impactful projects and social media statistics. The club was also able to spread its wings in serving the communities within and beyond boundaries to a substantial extent.

“Turning a crisis into an opportunity” is the key motive all of us adhered to from the start of the year. From end to end brainstorming sessions and equipping various approaches to innovate projects, we were able to implement and execute a total of 104 impactful projects. Introducing new initiatives with a high sustainability and surging the scope of the projects with a history were two main goals we were able to attain in this year.

Despite the various hardships that have come in our way, we were able to make myriad memories, strengthen our bonds, foster new friendships, and extend our support to everyone in every possible way.

The reason behind all the accomplishments, what I firmly believe is the exceptional leadership of president Rtr. Malkine Lokuge, ever so hardworking and talented board of directors and dedicated members of RACIIT. It was always a gratifying sight to spectate “We all work together. Like a big family” during every single occasion of the club. Not to forget about the immense amount of help fostered to us by the Teacher-in-Charge of the Rotaract Club of IIT Mrs. Gayashini Rathnayake, District steering committee 2020-21 lead by DRR.Rtn.Rtr.PP. Kasun Sigera, Guide Club Coordinator, Rtn.Rtr.IPP.Ranga Samarakoon and Immediate Past President Rtr. Pasan Ganewatte. It was an absolute pleasure to have been guided by all of you throughout the past year. I would also thank my parents and my sisters for the undying support they have given all throughout the Rotaract journey at IIT.

To have served in the Rotaract Club of IIT for the past three years, was definitely the most momentous period of my life. Memories, friends, learning to cherish little things, experiencing little moments worth more than what I’ll earn in a lifetime, are a few things Rotaract offered me, of which I am always grateful for.

As a new era dawns on RACIIT, I wish President-Elect Rtr. Induwara Mapalagama and Secretary-Elect Rtr. Umeshaa Sivakumar all the best of luck in taking RACIIT to reach greater standards.

Viva La Rotaract!

Rtr. Amesha Vithanage
Secretary 2020-21
Rotaract Club of IIT.
