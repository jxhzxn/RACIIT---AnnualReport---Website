Avenue Ranking

“Rank does not confer privilege or power. It imposes responsibility.” There are many reasons as to why grading or ranking systems exist in the education system and corporate world. Rank is the position that any individual or a group of individuals secure based on a given criteria. A good rank allows many opportunities, it boosts one’s confidence and it also allows one to improve themselves.
Rotaract Club of IIT comprises seven avenues, Club Service, Community service, Public Relations, International Services, Professional Development, Information and Technology, Sports and Finance.
We graded the avenues based on the efforts, the amount of work concluded, and the active participation in other avenue work and towards the end of each month, the avenue which scored the most points was awarded as the avenue of the month. Because of this rank system we initiated, the enthusiastic and confident board of directors of RACIIT engaged in completion of the work in a less stressful but rather fun way. This initiative has undoubtedly improved the personalities of all the directors, and we can assure that these practices will definitely aid them in conducting their day-to-day activities in a smooth way. IMPACT: - Boost the confidence of the board members. - Appreciate the work done and completed every week. - To enhance being more strategic, plan the daily work,complete the work before the given deadlines and improve the communication of joint directors. - Incorporate these practices when organizing events and executing them.
