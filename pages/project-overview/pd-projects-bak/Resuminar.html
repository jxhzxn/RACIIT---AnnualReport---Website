Resuméinar

A curriculum vitae (CV) is a marketing document that gives a summary of a job seekers career history, academic qualifications and explains their future potential. This document is created and used by a person to present their background, skills, and accomplishments. Résumés can be used for a variety of reasons, but most often they are used to secure new employment. Resumes also help employers make hiring decisions.
The Rotaract Club of IIT initiated the first step to teach young Rotaractors and students the importance of writing a good Resume and how to write a professional resume which meets employment requirements. Project RESUMÉINAR under the Professional Development Avenue was held to educate everyone on the importance of an eye-catching CV. Educating the fundamental importance of a Resume and teaching them on how to properly prepare a professional Resume plays a vital part in the growing world of competition amongst the student body in securing employment.
This session was carried out virtually via google meets on the 7th of November 2020. Two professionals from Fortude, Dilina Gunathilake who is the Global Head of Talent Acquisition and Sasini Withana who is the Senior Executive of Fortude – Sri Lanka. Ms. Sasini gave a wonderful explanation about how each chapter in the CV should be written to enhance your professionalism and impress the committee. After Ms. Sasini’s explanation, Mr. Dilina held an interactive Q and A session which was very important and factually informative to all the participants. - Educate the youngsters on creating a “right” Curriculum Vitae when applying for a Job interview by providing a clear distinction between the do’s and don’ts, tips and tricks . - Q&A session to answer the Questions proposed by the participants.
