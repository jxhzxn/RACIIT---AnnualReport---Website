Aftermath

The project was initiated by the rotaract clubs of ICBT, IIT and University of Colombo Faculty of Arts. The idea began with wanting to make a series of episodes that would show how youth get by in modern life and the types of issues they face with their parents, friends, future etc. However, due to the pandemic we couldn't shoot the episodes as planned. So it was decided to go with a short film that would highlight one of the major issues currently faced by many; the mental health issues caused by Covid - 19.
The film begins with the passing away of a young mans’ dad which leads him to be depressed. Although his friends try to help he does not accept their help. This results in him losing his job and friends because of the lack of understanding by his workplace and colleagues which also causes him to have financial issues. He has a sick mother who needs regular medication and due to the financial issues he was unable to afford to take care of his mother and he fell into debt which he failed to pay back. With all of this happening throughout the pandemic he does not find any hope to hold on to life and he decides to take his own life. The message we plan to give to the public is that everyone is going through their fair share of problems right now as a result of this pandemic and now more than ever was citizen of the country and humans need to step up and help those in need and check up on them and make sure whatever they are going through they won't have to go through it alone. The clubs have decided to continue this project next year as well while making necessary additions and improvements to have a greater impact on the society. We hope to have a larger and more elaborate storyline along with partnering up with an organization to ensure the impact on society.
