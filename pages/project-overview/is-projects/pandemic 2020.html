Pandemic 2020

The covid19 pandemic is the biggest global crisis our generation is currently facing. This is not just a health crisis ,but also a crisis which adversely affects the socio-economic aspects globally.

Focusing on this matter, Rotaract Club of IIT in Collaboration with Rotaract Club of Osmangazi, from Turkey conducted an International Services project called Pandemic 2020, in order to create awareness among people about how the global pandemic has affected different aspects such as economic, political, social and health areas globally. This project took place on Zoom - an online platform.

For this initiative, we invited a couple of speakers from foreign countries as well as from Sri Lanka. Our speakers were Dr, Thushani Dabrera, who is a member of the Sri Lanka medical council and Public health forum, a Life member of college of community Physicians and the Sri Lanka Medical Association, a Consultant Community Physician Regional Director of Health Services, Puttalam, represented Sri Lanka, whilst Rtr.Ege Ecem Parali, from Rotaract Club ood Osmangazi, who is a student studying at Sabanci University, Turkey, spoke about the real situation in their country. Dr. Daniel Bouzon Nagem Assad and Ms. Ana Carolina Vasconcelos are two research scientists in a reputed laboratory in Brazil, who also shared their thoughts regarding the Covid 19 situation in their country. The final speaker was Mr.Rafael de la Vega, who is an economist, a lecturer of Economics and an industrial engineer at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ).

Project Impact

  • Spread awareness concerning social, economic, political issues faced by different countries globally.
  • Create awareness among people on how to deal during a global crisis.
  • Provide insights to the audience, mostly youngsters on how to rely on a situation like this and work for the betterment of the world.
