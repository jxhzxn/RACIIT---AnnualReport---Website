DCM - May

The District Council Meeting is a gathering in which the District Steering Committee briefs on the projects and other updates to the council which comprises the Presidents and Secretaries of the Rotaract Clubs in RID 3220.

The District Council Meeting is held in the first week of every month. In this meeting, the District Rotaract Representative, the District Rotaract Chair and the District Steering Committee give overall updates of the new initiatives and various other important matters. Club updates are also given by the representatives (Presidents, Secretaries, or proxies) who are present during the meeting.

District Council Meeting for the month of May, was held on the 2nd of May 2021. It was hosted by the Rotaract Clubs of Achievers Lanka Business School, Informatics Institute of Technology and Wattala. This was conducted via Zoom virtual platform due to the Covid-19 outbreak. The meeting commenced sharp at 10:30 a.m., and it concluded at 1:30 p.m. in which various important and significant updates were given by the DRR, DRC and District Steering Committee on the goals achieved as a movement throughout the year and precisely cleared the final tasks that each club had to complete before the stipulated deadlines.

After the updates of each club, the Presidents of the month for April were announced. Before the conclusion of the meeting, a video clip that consisted of entertaining and momentous captures of the District Council 2020-21 was played.

Project Impact

  • Clubs getting an update on the ongoing progress of their fellow clubs.
  • A time of fellowship amongst all the club representatives and the district steering committee.
