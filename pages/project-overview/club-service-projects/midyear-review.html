Mid year Review

The mid-year review was a project that we organized in order to look into the progress of all the Avenues of our Club and the measures we must take in order to improve the work of our respective avenues as a whole. We discussed many important aspects throughout this gathering. Having such important conversations among the board members helped us to envision ourselves as a movement that would continuously use our strength and strong friendships as a key in achieving greater heights while uplifting our own community, alongside opening opportunities for our Rotaractors to grow and improve their skills as well, thus resulting in an efficient group of young individuals who will be able to successfully thrive in society, making our community an impactful place. It is almost needless to say that these are the small things that will actually help us grow both professionally and personally as well.

We were further able to get a good understanding about where exactly we as a non-profit organization stand and how our projects impact our communities, ethnicities and cultures as a whole. And we were able to realize the change and impact we can bring on to our society through the work and projects that we do and how it can brighten up the lives of people in need. And most importantly we were able to understand the true potential that lies within all of us and how we can use it to improve and impact the society in a better way, not to mention, we also learnt that together, we can strive and learn as one united non-profit service organization.

Project Impact

  • We were able to get a clear insight into each of the avenues and about their progress as well as the downfalls, and what exactly we should do in order to increase the value and scalability of our projects.
  • We also got a good idea on how to execute projects and how to plan a project in such a way that the output of the project is truly important and valuable in terms of sustainability.
  • As it's said, “teamwork is dream work,” this day proved this statement right as we learnt that together we can achieve everything and even more than what the club has achieved so far.
  • The long-term impact of this project was giving all 20 of us a boost for the next 6 months. We have strived for 6 months with many ups and downs and this project gave a boost for the rest of our period in the club.
