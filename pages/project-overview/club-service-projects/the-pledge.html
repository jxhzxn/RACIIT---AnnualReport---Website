The Pledge

The Rotaract club of IIT, organized “The Pledge”, an online magic show for the first time in the history of the club. The event was held on the 30th of November via zoom and commenced at 6.30pm. We were able to witness an astonishing performance by Sumangala Silva, known as creator of the first and only Educational Magic show of Sri Lanka and a semifinalist at Sri Lanka's Got talent. Mr. Silva is the first and only Sri Lankan magician so far to qualify (got sanctioned by IBM) to compete in the World Championship of Magic. He served as an event officer and Event Manager in Sri Lanka and in many other countries and was the 3-time National Champion of Magic. In addition to his mind-blowing magic tricks, he also promoted the importance of conserving the environment by his performance.

The project was a one day show which lasted for two hours with a crowd of 150-200 participants. The participants include our very own RACIIT members and fellow colleagues along with members from other Rotaract clubs. Some may even say that hosting an online magic show might be challenging and unsuccessful, but the fellowship created , and the turnout of the event proved it wrong.

Ever since the deteriorating rate of Covid-19 in the country, we had to follow strict movement restrictions and frequent lockdowns which resulted in staying connected online. With time, most of us were getting bored stiff and lost motivation. Hence, the club service avenue proposed a new initiative, to give the participants a sense of enthusiasm and eagerness.

The pledge was successfully concluded on the 30th of November with tons of good feedback from the participants which gave us nothing but pure joy and satisfaction.

Project Impact

  • It was a good opportunity for all participants to spend their time productively and safely following all health guidelines necessary during this pandemic period.
  • This project was smoothly executed. Educated participants about the environment through Mr. Silva’s acts which we hope everyone follows.
  • The total number of participants reached almost 200 making it beyond the expected limit given the limited time to publicize the event. The live chat showcased that the people were thoroughly fascinated by his tricks and his fans were in absolute awe.
