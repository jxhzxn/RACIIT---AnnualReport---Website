Intro to RACIIT

The key to a strong superstructure is a solid foundation, without a solid foundation, there will be trouble trying to create anything of value. So, in order to have a solid foundation, there is a need for clear clarification to be made. In the terms of the Rotaract Club of IIT the foundation and driving force are our general members and as the directors and the executive committee, it is our responsibility to present a clear idea of what the Rotaract movement is about and what we do as RACIITians.

It is a responsibility carried to explain and clarify any doubts the member may have and to build strong bonds beyond our differences. As an institutional-based club every new academic intake rings the arrival of new and fresh energy into the RACIIT family. In order to welcome these fresh minds into the family and to help identify each individual and area of expertise the RACIIT conducts micro projects, allowing us to get to know the new member’s capabilities while giving them a chance to settle into the RACIIT environment.However, this year due to the current situation and the limitations on physical gatherings this was not possible. This being the reason we took a turn on the processes and hence, organized this session aiming to share our Rotaract knowledge with them and explain in detail the importance of being part of the Rotaract community.

Project Impact

  • Got to know the new members and to share the idea of Rotaract in the district 3220 and how RACIIT stands out allowing them to feel knowledgeable and included in the family. This was mainly due to the absence of physical interaction with the members due to the current global situation.
  • Confirmation of the new members who are willing to be actively involved in projects over the years.
  • It was also the most effective way of educating the new members on the procedure and practices of the RACIIT with which we began to include them in project committees.
