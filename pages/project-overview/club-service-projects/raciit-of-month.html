RACIITIAN’s of the month

“Appreciation is a wonderful thing. It makes what’s excellent in others belong to us as well.`` A famous saying quoted by Voltaire emphasizes the importance of appreciating someone’s effort.The whole world is running so fast that we all are stuck in a nutshell where we tend to notice the weaknesses of another person in something he or she does. But never do we think how that affects that person’s mindset. One word of appreciation would encourage a person to become a better version of himself.

RACIIT in a rather smart way chose to go in a different path and notice the good in people rather than pointing out their insecurities or weaknesses.As an initiative commencing from within the club itself, RACIIT initiated the project named “RACIITIANS OF THE MONTH”.

The sole purpose of this project is to award a title of appreciation for the hard working individuals of RACIIT who leave prominent marks of success in every project they do without anticipating any personal comeback.At the first glance one would say this whole idea sounds shallow, but anyone who is aware of the burden an undergraduate bears during the few years at university would always know the load of work they’re supposed to complete with deadlines assigned. Therefore anyone who spares a considerably unusual amount of time at any expense on getting a project done successfully is definitely worthy of appreciation since he or she is sacrificing the time they could have put into use for self-development.

Project Impact

  • The members are aware of this title and that their performance and versatility is being kept track of. As a result they always try to do whatever the task assigned to them in the best way possible.
  • At the end of the month when the appreciation poster circulates on social media the title bearers would receive wishes from their mates and that would always be an incentive to drive them to perform better in the upcoming projects. Though this particular outcome seems shallow, this self-confidence and esteem that comes to a person’s mind once their work is appreciated is priceless.
  • A driving force for personal and professional self-development since it encourages the members’ confidence and participation in the projects we organize.
  • This initiative accelerates the importance of coming forward and utilizing one's skills to do successful things and how grooming personal and professional skills could be of immense prominence for now as well as for the future.
