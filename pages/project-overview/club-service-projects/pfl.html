PFL

Futsal is a sport which teaches an individual what it takes to succeed. From months or even decades of tough practices and training, many have gained a hard work ethic. From fellow teammates one will learn how to work well with others in a group. But most importantly, being in a sports team brings about the togetherness in everyone which makes the bond that they hold stronger.

Pro Futsal League (PFL) is a futsal tournament initiated by the Rotaract Club of IIT for the second consecutive year, only this year we implemented it on a larger scale, involving around 17 clubs from around Sri Lanka to bring fellow Rotaractors together in fellowship. As much as we like our club members working together within the club, we also love to see them interacting with fellow Rotaractors so that the bond between our club and theirs remains strong and whole forever. With this objective in mind, we initiated the Pro Futsal League (PFL) under the club service avenue.

PFL had two major important events. One was the Auction Day which took place at HomeTree Coworking space in Bambalapitiya and the other was the final Tournament which took place in Club Fusion Boralesgamuwa .

The Auction Day or the player selection process was held on the 12th of March where players were selected by the 8 managers who were representatives of the District Council. Once the teams were finalized, we hosted the tournament on the 28th of March, where all these 8 teams battled it out for the ultimate grand prize. The tournament concluded on the same day with an awarding ceremony which consisted of awards such as certificates for the participants, Champion’s trophy, runners up trophy and individual awards for the player of the tournament, Golden Boot and Best Goalkeeper.

The project, which was sponsored by Multilac, Pepsi, RBN, The Papare.com, Villa Perpetua, computer mania, was concluded by making it an impactful, fun, and successful two month-long project which was centered around 64 players and reached out to over 1000+ people. Moreover, apart from PFL being one of the very few initiatives to bring many clubs together after a long time owing to the uncertainty the pandemic has brought upon the country, it contributed immensely towards membership development within the members of the club and building fellowship with other clubs.

Project Impact

  • Through this project we were able to gather most of the clubs and we have received considerably a large amount of excellent feedback from the event as well.
  • Members learn the value of teamwork, time management, event planning, etc.
  • We believe that we were able to provide them with a platform to make brand new friendships, memories, and desires.
  • The feedback received was priceless and as an organizing committee we were left with absolute pride and tears of joy. As Rtr.PP.Kayalvili Mathavaram , team manager of Tuskers said “It was a brilliant idea to gather all the clubs and get them to engage Rotaract while we all strive as one”, it was indeed a league to remember and we will be looking forward to see this successful initiative being implemented year after year.
  • Our efforts were mainly oriented towards giving the players a league to remember especially after almost a year or so of lockdown and isolation. We believe that we were able to provide them with a platform to make brand new friendships, memories, and desires
