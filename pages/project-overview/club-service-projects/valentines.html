Project Valentines

Chocolates, flowers, love notes—what’s not to like about Valentine’s Day? Valentine’s Day is celebrated every 14th of February by numerous people across the world where candy, flowers and gifts are exchanged between loved ones, all in the name of St. Valentine. It’s a common day for friends and lovers to exchange small tokens of affection or handwritten notes, cards, gifts, and treats. You can show love to your family, friends, and spouse any day, but Valentine’s Day is a fun reminder of all the ways we can show love to the people in our lives.

Bearing this in mind, the Club Service avenue of the Rotaract Club of Informatics Institute of Technology decided to do something special to help spread the joy of valentines among all those who are celebrating.Our target being all those who got that special someone they would like to treat on valentine’s day, we had the perfect sweets and treats just for them and their loved ones.

The main aim behind this project was to raise funds for all our community service-based projects and hence the goodies that were bought had to be sold keeping a profit. Throughout the day we made our way to Galle Face, Viharamahadevi Park, Bellanwila walking and cycling park, Independence Arcade and numerous other most favorite hot spots around Colombo and sold freshly baked treats. Additionally, we also took pre-orders from people we knew so that we could deliver the goodies right to their doorstep. The day which began at 8.00am in the morning was successfully completed by 9.00pm making it yet another memorable but profitable Valentine’s Day for not just us, but even our customers.

Project Impact

  • The project helped spread the joy of the valentine’s celebrations while also raising funds for the club’s activities.
  • Apart from which we also contributed an additional revenue to all the small businesses that the items we sold were purchased from.
  • It was possible to teach the club members on how projects are planned and executed and were also given a firsthand experience with the possible hindrances that may occur when doing so and how to overcome them which will benefit them individually and the club in the future.
  • By purchasing the baked goods from small businesses, we were also able to promote their product to all those who bought our items throughout the day which will in the long run benefit them as their products have now reached out to a wider range of people.
  • The revenue collected is utilized in the community service projects which will create a colossal amount of impact to the ones in need.
