Survive

SURVIVE was an Among Us tournament, open only to the members of the Rotaract Club of IIT. Selected as the game of choice for the tournament because of its popularity among many people, the games were conducted via the online application Among Us, with Discord being the platform for verbal communication and interaction during the games.
The tournament took place on the 20th of December 2020 in 4 rounds: the qualifiers, quarterfinals, semifinals, and finals. With a total of 32 RACIIT members in participation, the tournament went on for a duration of 10 hours- starting at 11 am and ending at 8 pm- with breaks between each round. In each round, the competitors had to participate in a total of four games: 1 Run and Catchers round, 1 Hide and Seek Round, and 2 classic games, according to the point system prepared by the Chairperson of the project. The tournament also consisted of 4 to 5 moderators, either members or volunteers of the project. One moderator was present in every game to monitor the players and ensure the tournament rules and regulations were followed by the players , help calculate the points, determine the final scores and oversee the overall proceedings of the game.
Ever since lockdown was imposed and institutions had to temporarily shut down all activities, hosting meetings or any physical project has been a challenge. However, we made the best of every opportunity we got online to bring our members together and create new bonds. Project impact ● The project allowed the participants to have some fun and put aside the stresses of the pandemic by getting to know their fellow club members better and engage in some friendly competition. ● A small escape from all the stress our workload is throwing at us can bring relief and pleasure. The main reason it was open to only RACIITians was to give them an opportunity to escape the stress caused by the heavy stress load. ● the formation of stronger bonds between the club members, and increased networking. ● As among us is a multi-player game, this requires teamwork and getting to know the members of your team. Getting to know people results in being able to develop social skills which could be beneficial when going out into the professional world. ● Additionally, the game will bring about the critical thinking and decision-making skills of the participants. Which also promotes social skills and self-development. Secondary Avenue- Fellowship Chairperson- Adeeb Gazzali , Paickeer Mohommed Start Date -2020-12-01 End Date -2020-12-20
