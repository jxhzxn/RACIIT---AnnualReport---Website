Hari bn ela

Victor Borge once said that “laughter is the shortest distance between two people”. Sometimes, we all need a good laugh, a reason to smile and the cure of happiness which most of the time brings people closer together.

Although stand up comedy is a well-liked performance internationally, in Sri Lanka it is not given as much significance. However, there are many Sri Lankans, both Men and women who have stretched the limits of comedy with the boldness of their imagination as it takes talent, courage, and boldness for one to grab an audience’s attention, let alone, making them all laugh. Which is why the Rotaract Club of IIT, being a club that strives to bring out the best in people, giving exposure and recognition, bought in the Club service initiative ‘Hari bn ela’, with the sole motive of giving a stage for talent to shine whilst focusing on fellowship and membership development.

The project entailed a night, comprising comedic entertainment held at the Central Perk on the 9th of February. It consisted of stand-up comedians, who volunteered to entertain the crowd within a period of three hours. Additionally, Mr. Kapila Rasnayaka, a well-known stand-up comedian in Sri Lanka, also graced the occasion and left the crowd highly entertained.

There was never a moment where we did not experience any laughter and all the jokes made by the comedians left the crowd in tears of laughter. Apart from all the fun and laughter there was also fellowship and making new friendships as this initiative was one of the few projects to be held physically, considering the ongoing COVID crisis restrictions. As a final note, the number of participants attending for the event was beyond expected and the entire project was carried out by abiding by the health and safety guidelines provided to reduce the spread of the COVID-19. Even though our numbers were restricted, without a single doubt we could say that every single participant departed with a smile on their face after an exultant and fulfilling day.

Project Impact

  • Our main aim which was to create a night of comedic relief for the audience was accomplished with a terrific participant count.
  • We were able to increase the unity between members and to increase the engagement of the club members so that everyone has a chance to showcase their leadership skills, strategizing skills, and project management skills.
  • The feedback received from our audience was priceless, needless to say that the price for a ticket was extremely worth it and all the funds received was given out to our community service-based projects.
