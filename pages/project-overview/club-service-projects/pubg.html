Pubg Mobile 2020

Although it’s said to be known that gaming is merely a sport and nothing but a distraction and an addictive source of entertainment, recent research has proved that gaming has numerous benefits that contribute to developing the cognitive skills in both adults and children.Just as physical activity contributes to strengthening muscles, games stimulates the brain to indulge in strenuous activity for a long period of time.Likewise, there are many advantages gaming can bring.

Therefore, the sports avenue of the Rotaract Club of IIT hosts an annual gaming festival- IGF, in order to embrace e-sports in Sri lanka. Prior to this event, we hosted a mini PUBG battle for all the PUBG fans out there in order to raise funds for the bigger battle. The PUBG mobile tournament was held on the 31st of July and commenced at around 7. 00pm.The tournament was held virtually, and the contestants were asked to register in teams of 4 players per team. PUBG Mobile took place this time for the second consecutive year and was open to anyone, including RACIITians. A small entry fee of Rs.300 per team was charged to collect cash for the prize pool and luckily, due to the number of registrations, we were able to collect a profit out of this project. After a phase of nerve wrecking excitement and intense competition among the teams, the event was brought to an end at around 11.00pm, after announcing the winning team and the most valuable player of the tournament.

Project Impact

  • Provide gamers a chance to showcase their talents and skills as It’s very rare that gamers get the chance to compete in order to use their skills.
  • Reaching out to other club members to join in for a time of fellowship, whilst investing their time on something worthwhile.
