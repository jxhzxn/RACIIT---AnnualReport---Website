Mistletoe & Win

“The world has grown weary through the years, but at Christmas, it is young.” - Phillips Brooks The holidays, a period almost everyone looks forward to. Especially with the month of November drawing to an end, and Christmas just around the corner, there’s a great deal of rushing and excitement in houses, streets, neighborhoods, villages and cities.However, this Christmas was slightly different for all of us.

So, just like all the other virtual events brought to you by the Rotaract club of IIT during the period of quarantine to keep everybody occupied and members active, as the club service avenue, we decided to conduct an interactive Christmas trivia Night with the intention of building fellowship and a sense of togetherness. The Christmas trivia was named “Mistletoe and Win” and was conducted via the online platform -Quizizz. The competition was open to both Rotaractors and non-Rotaractors.

The preliminary round in which all the registered participants took part consisted of thirty challenging Christmas themed questions. Immediately after the completion of the initial round, a second round was carried out to select the top three winners out of the top twenty contestants shortlisted from the initial round. In order to make the game challenging in comparison to the first round, the second round was composed of harder questions so that it would be easier to eliminate. The trivia questions were created to make the game challenging while providing interesting facts about Christmas one may have never known. The platform was convenient enough to provide an interactive and user-friendly session throughout the trivia.

At the end of the second round the top three winners were chosen and they were awarded with appetizing Christmas goodies sponsored by “theconfectionary.sl”Mistletoe and win was hosted on the 27th of December for the very first time in the history of the Rotaract Club of IIT

Project Impact

  • Mistletoe and Win became a perfect success as it was able to provide an interactive trivia night to the competitors under the safest conditions. It was ideal for lifting up the Christmas spirit of the participants who were probably stuck in their homes in boredom for the Christmas season.
  • Mistletoe and Win was able to provide Christmas related knowledge to the students from other religions as well and it also was an ideal opportunity for the participants to show off their interest on Christmas.
  • It was a great chance to get to know how enthusiastic the people are for Christmas events, which in return encouraged us to come up with more exciting events in the upcoming year as well.
