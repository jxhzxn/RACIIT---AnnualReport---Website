IGF

Video games are so often misinterpreted as unsophisticated, immature or ‘the domain of couch potatoes.’ But did you know that many common elements of these simulated worlds can provide substantial benefits in real life?

ESports is one of the most prominent areas that has been making an appearance in the sporting arena during the last few years, even in countries like Sri Lanka. The Rotaract Club of IIT, being a club of an IT based institute initiated “IGF'' or IIT gaming fest, under the club service avenue, back in 2016 to take on this new trend in the world of sports and to give a chance for all passionate young gamers in Sri Lanka to showcase their hidden skill and talent in the fast-paced field of E-Sports. IGF is a proud initiative that has succeeded over the years to redefine the ESports platform in Sri Lanka.

IGF was held for the 5th consecutive year and brought in the concept of a unique, hybrid version this year as the COVID-19 outburst compelled all citizens to abide by all the health guidelines imposed by the health authorities of Sri Lanka. Therefore, this year we focused on providing a safe platform for the participants to compete in an E-Sports championship comprising highly acclaimed titles such as Valorant, DOTA 2, PUBG Mobile which were carried out virtually and FIFA ’21 carried out physically at Game edge, spanning over to promote competitive game play on PC, Mobile and Cross Platform. All games were held under the said timelines of the competition dates, where players joined virtually and challenged each other to friendly competition and gradually, some of which emerged as champions.

With every passing year, there has been an improvement in the project scope. This gaming tournament is now a well-known tournament in the gaming community and the registrations received said otherwise. IGF 2021 was concluded with over 750+ registrations, 440+ participants and an audience of over 1200+ viewers from across Sri Lanka inclusive of a cumulative prize pool of LKR 60,000 covering all 4 major titles - Valorant, DOTA 2, PUBG Mobile and FIFA 21’.

Project Impact

  • This project reached out to all those upcoming gamers out there who needed a platform to shine.
  • IGF is a fairly large-scale event , and we were able to get participants from different areas of Sri Lanka.
  • As it was held virtually. Therefore, we believe we may have had a huge impact and we hope that we were successful in promoting the eSports industry of Sri Lanka.
  • In total, we received an overall number of 450 participant registrations,400 participants and 1200+ spectators from all days. Despite having one game less than that of last year, we managed to gain more participants this year, contributing towards the project goal of creating awareness concerning eSports in Sri Lanka.
