RACIIT V-logs

“As a daily vlogger, I can go back and relive so many great moments at anytime” Through RACIIT vlogs which have been uploaded frequently we are able to show everyone a glimpse of what we've been upto in the past 365 days.

“when it comes to vlogging , the content of your vlog is much more important than what you film on” RACIIT vlogs aims to not only showcase the projects which the club has been working on but also the fun side of RACIIT through the club trips, fellowship visits , annual board outing vlogs and also the throwback content and other series such as “Never Have I Ever ” .

  • The first Vlog “VLOG 01” highlighted the first ever Inter-Board Futsal/Cricket Tournament .
  • “VLOG 02” featured highlights from the month of July.
  • “VLOG 03” proudly presents “Beach Yaris” , another video filled with memes and more delightful content.
  • “VLOG 04” is yet another exciting vlog filled with sudden decisions and more food
  • “VLOG 05 - Wayamba Visit - The Aftermovie” is an eventful sneak peak of the Wayamba district fellowship .
  • “VLOG 06” brings back memories from the Badulla Fellowship Visit.
  • “ VLOG O7” brings back those memories with a small glimpse of some behind the scenes.
  • “VLOG 08” - Board Outing” is the first vlog for the year 2021 .
  • “VLOG 09” - Throwback” is all about bringing back some of the fun and exciting moments of RACIIT .
  • “VLOG 10”- RACIIT ON CRACK” is yet another vlog to spice up your day and this is one special vlog.
  • ”VLOG 11” - The Ride Was Fun” brings you back all the fun memories from the year.


Project Impact

  • Provide a glimpse of the club activities that were carried out in each month in a fun and joyful approach; vlogs.
  • Create a platform to share the activities conducted by the club to the general public.
