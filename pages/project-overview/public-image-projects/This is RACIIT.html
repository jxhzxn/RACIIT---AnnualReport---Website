This is RACIIT

The PR avenue launched the project ThisIsRACIIT which aimed to focus on the positive energy brought by the projects and activities conducted together and it’s spread for others. “This is RACIIT” was an annual project which was conducted every year at RACIIT.This initially consisted of all the fun filled, informative and knowledge transfer activities we conducted for the members after a general meeting. Unfortunately due to the Covid-19 outbreak continuing this project , was a question.After so many brainstorming sessions we finalised our approach.i.e to incorporate the 21st century’s most trending app, Tik Tok.

Since most of our projects were conducted online involving virtual engagement we initiated a step to level up our initiative in a more enthusiastic manner. That is, to spread the wings by being the first club in the RID 3220 to create and share content of what we do as a club involving members, in the most evolving social media platform recorded up to date; Tik Tok.

ThisIsRaciit was initiated mid December 2020 and we kept on creating small one minute / 30 second videos with the ongoing trends crawling off on social media. The mission was to show how fun and merry can working together be and it’s opportunity to grow within the community of the Rotaract club of IIT. We also introduced the hashtag, #thisisRACIIT which is , we are proud to say, has gained a major popularity amongst the youth out there and also has become one of the trending hashtags in the past few months.

Project Impact

  • Initiate a new approach to execute the project “This is RACIIT” by incorporating Tik Tok application .
  • Create and share content of what we do as a club involving members.
  • Show how fun and merry can working together be and its opportunity to grow within the community of the Rotaract club of IIT.
  • Introduce a new social media platform amongst the rotaract community to promote what we do as a movement.
