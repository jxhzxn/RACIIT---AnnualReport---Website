Shutter Up_SL

This was a joint initiative between Rotaract clubs across RID 3220, initiated spearheaded by RAC Trincomalee , RAC Moratuwa , RAC IIT, RAC ACBT, RAC NSBM, RAC central campus & RAC J.Pura.

This online photography competition was envisioned as a public image activity to bolster the competitive spirit among Rotaractors and to create online chatter about rotaract movement. The open competition invited participants to send their best photographs to be put up in a social media page created especially for this. The photos were then ranked on the basis of their popularity and based on a number of select judges' choices. As it was the inaugural year, only an E- certificate will be provided to the winners.

Photography allows us to glimpse things we would not have noticed otherwise. Photography allows you to visually present your thoughts to others. Competitions may be an excellent method to instill some discipline in projects. The submission procedure itself can inspire greater project articulation and allow photographers to analyze their work more objectively. Furthermore, solo shooting may be a scary experience at times, especially for new photographers or those working on long-term projects. Award contests can serve as a type of "third-party approbation," giving practitioners a boost of confidence, recognition, and, in some cases, much needed support.

Project Impact

  • Instill some discipline in projects.
  • Allow photographers to analyze their work more objectively.
  • Promote fellowship and enhance building bonds with the collaborating rotaract clubs.
  • Provide a platform for the photographers to showcase their talents.
