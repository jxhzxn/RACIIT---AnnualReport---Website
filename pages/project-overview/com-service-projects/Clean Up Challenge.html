Clean up Challenge

“Cleanup Challenge” is an initiative by the Rotaract Clubs of IIT, ACBT and Colombo West, who as young Rotaractors instigated a move to spread awareness about cleaning our surroundings and maintain its cleanliness.
This initiative was all about challenging the younger generation to clean up their surroundings in order to spread awareness on environment pollution. Due to the strict movement restrictions imposed by the country’s government given the ongoing pandemic crisis, we made the challenge even more unique by allocating a time slot for all Rotaractors and non-Rotaractors to come together and clean their surroundings, indicating that no matter the obstacle, as responsible citizens we would still come together to take care of something bigger than ourselves.
Putting in a joint effort, we released a poster including the challenge information such as the deadline to send the needful pictures and the time of initiation. After a while, we received submissions of many youngsters actively engaging in cleaning their surroundings.
After successfully accomplishing our goal, we released a video of all the participant entries from different parts of Sri Lanka, to create awareness on how important it is to conserve our planet and how together, no matter the barrier, we can still make a difference. Project impact ● This brought out a sense of responsibility to keep their homes and neighborhood clean. The visible results may lead to heightened interest and involvement among the community to preserve and keep the environment clean.
