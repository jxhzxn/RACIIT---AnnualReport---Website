Podcast

Technology has turned into a major need in modern society. Looking into the present society and the way the world works it is impossible to have a proper education without the use of the internet and technology especially after the pandemic hit. People still lack the knowledge and aren’t yet familiar with how modern technology works. Which is why we came up with an initiative to preach about the importance and the worth of technology through a podcast series.
This podcast named ‘TechMeOut’ produces the idea of preaching the importance and advantages as well it’s other experiences about technology. Since podcasting is a medium allowing students flexible access to teaching materials. Podcasts give students a choice about where and when they would like to listen. Apart from the convenience of following at your own pace, podcasts accommodate audio-based learning styles, which might specifically suit those Currently we have released two podcasts about technology over the span of one year. Moreover, this is a long-term project which plans to be continued over the years to come, resulting in a positive audience and minimizing the digital division that exists in the country. IMPACT : - Provide a platform to know anything about technology to everyone. - Reduce the digital divide that exists in the nation and create a better positive audience with the knowledge of modern technology.
