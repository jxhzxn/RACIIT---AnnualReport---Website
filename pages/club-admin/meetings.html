Meetings

Board Meetings

Procedure of the Board Meeting

The board along with the executive committee is the driving force of a club. Whilst bearing the responsibility of making sure the club serves its purpose to the community, ensuring the members make the best of their Rotaract career, the board of the Rotaract Club of IIT together endeavors to go that extra mile to be a beacon to the Rotaract community.

Having set apart Tuesdays for board meetings, board meetings are held every once a week, making it four times a month. This is where the director board gathers to give in avenue updates. These updates include new ideas for projects, updates on ongoing projects, discuss on arising problems related to the club, select the RACIITians of the month, and most importantly, create memories. When it comes to new ideas, the irrespective avenue directors propose their ideas during the meeting, the rest of the board is free to add on any comments and the executive committee gives their feedback and approval on how to develop the idea further. Although coming up with new ideas is mandatory for each avenue at the beginning of the month, most arising ideas are given just as it arises, sometimes even suggested by the members. The whole purpose of weekly meetings is to check in the progress of projects under each avenue and the executive committee gives in their advice and points out changes if necessary. With the completion of a project, the directors are always appreciated for their hard work during these meetings as well. Additionally, in every board meeting in the last week of the month we select the RACIITians of the month through a voting process to make the selection process fair. Every meeting is always concluded with a time of fellowship, making board meetings not only a place to review and develop projects but also a day to look forward to.

General Meetings

Procedure of the General Meeting

General meetings are where all the members of the Rotaract Club of IIT come together every twice a month either virtually or when possible, physically in fellowship to update themselves on the ongoing state of the club. The meetings are usually started off with avenue updates given by the irrespective avenues and their project committees. The board encourages the committee members of projects (General members) to give updates at general meetings to help them develop their soft skills. Following the process of avenue updates, we then move on to an overall update by the executive committee and we never fail to conduct an activity to induce fellowship amongst the members. These activities most often include a performance by our very own members, ice breakers, fun team building challenges and if conducted physically, games that require every member to be engaged.

Elaborating more on General meetings, these customary meetings have been carried out every year where the first meeting of every intake would be interactive, and members must be highly engaged. Starting off with the introductory session, where one would be required to tell her name, age, school, degree, and to add a little spice to it he or she had to share her relationship status which always leaves everyone highly enthusiastic, displaying the fun side of RACIIT. General Meetings never go by without it being a fun time of fellowship, making it a comfortable place for anyone to fit in, a place where one can call home.
