2012/13 - Rtr. Jehan De Silva

Highlights

  • Year theme: Spread Smiles.
  • Started HDS in the college Common Room. Partnering up with Bairaha.

2013/14 - Rtr. Dilshan Ranesh Kottegoda

Highlights

  • Year Theme: Building equality through friendship.
  • HDS was conducted in Walkers Café.
  • Our most recognized project Miracle was executed in this year with a total raise of 1.5 million.

Awards

  • Community Service Gold Award.
  • Bronze Award for HDS.
×

2014/15 - Rtr. Punith Gunasekara

Highlights

  • Year theme: Fostering Multicultural unity.
  • HDS was conducted in Police Park.
  • Our signature IU project of hosting Rotaractors from Hong Kong was started in this year.

Awards

  • International Service Silver Award.
×

2015/16 - Rtr. Shenal Mendis

Highlights

  • Year Theme: Voice for the Voiceless.
  • HDS had its biggest break. HDS was taken to Racecourse and was made a public extravaganza.
  • Another signature project WeTalk was started in this year.

Awards

  • HDS - PR Silver Award.
  • WeTalk – International Understanding Bronze Award.
×

2016/17 - Rtr. Rumesh Thiruchchelvan

Highlights

  • Year theme: Engage.Lead.Sustain.
  • The Logo was standardized.
  • HDS became the signature project of the club and happened in Racecourse in an extensive scale.
  • HDS Project Chair: Rtr. Thareef Bin Ubaid & Rtr. Kusal Fernando.
  • RACIIT hosted the Conference.
  • Sally’s Manor was started as a Club Service initiative.
  • Club Service avenue functioned very well in raising a large Member base.

Awards

  • HDS - PR Silver Award.
  • WeTalk – International Understanding Bronze Award.
×

2017/18 - Rtr. Thareef Bin Ubaid

Highlights

  • Year theme: Friendship Serving Humanity.
  • HDS was promoted as a food festival and took place in Green Path.
  • HDS Project Chair: Rtr. Ashan Dissanayake.
  • Initiated Route 87 project with the Rotaract Club of Chunnakam.
  • In this year RACIIT became a well-known club among others in the Rotaract District 3220.
  • RACIIT won multiple awards and became the most outstanding Club for the first time in RACIIT History.
  • For the first time in History two members from RACIIT Appointed to the RID 3220Board. Rtr. Thareef Bin Ubaid (President) & Rtr. Thariq Ahamed (Secretary)

Awards

  • Most Outstanding Public Relations project: HDS Gold Award.
  • Most Outstanding Regional Engagement Effort: Gold Award.
  • Appreciation for Rotaract Golden Jubilee Celebrations: Gold Award.
  • Most Outstanding Club: Gold Awards
  • Most Outstanding project with Interactors: Trail Blazers Gold Award.
  • Most Outstanding Social Media Presence: Silver Award.
  • Outstanding Special Achievement on District Citation: Silver Award.
  • Most Outstanding Community Service project for Education: Striving for Miracle Silver Award.
  • Most Outstanding special project in Line with district: Route 87 Silver Award.
  • Most Outstanding Digital Communication Project: HaXmas Silver Award.
  • Most Outstanding President of the Year: Rtr. Thareef Bin Ubaid
×

2018/19 - Rtr. Vimukthi Gunaratna

Highlights

  • Year theme: Inspire through Experience.
  • HDS took place in Green Path which comprised of a food festival and a musical show after the competition.
  • For the first time in RACIIT History a project (HDS) had more than 25 sponsors.
  • HDS Project Chair: Rtr. Pasan Ganewatte.
  • Rtr. Muad Arshad (Sergeant at arms) initiated the project E-Mithudama which became an International Award-winning project and won 4 major Awards at the 29th Rotaract District Assembly.
  • Project E-Mithudama was nominated as the Best Community Service project in South Asia.
  • In this year RACIIT became a Club which maintained a good standard throughoutthe year by helping other clubs as well.
  • This was yet another successful year under Rtr. Vimukthi Gunarathna’s Presidency and became the Most Outstanding Club 2nd consecutive year in a row.

Awards

  • Most outstanding Club: Gold Award.
  • Rotaract South Asia Multi District Information Organization’s Outstanding Award: E-Mithudama Gold Award.
  • ‘INSPIRE THE FUTURE’ service excellence award: E-Mithudama Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Community Service Project on basic education and literacy: E-Mithudama Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Institute based participative Rotaract Club of the year: Gold Award.
  • President of the month for the month of October: Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Public Relations effort: HotDog Showdown 2019 Silver Award.
  • Most supportive Rotaract Club: Silver Award.
  • Most outstanding International Services award: Seva 1.0 Silver Award.
  • Most outstanding initiative focused on Sustainability of the year: E-Mithudama Silver Award.
  • Most outstanding membership development program: Silver Award.
  • Most outstanding Regional Engagement effort: Bronze Award.
  • Most Outstanding President of the Year: Rtr. Vimukthi Gunarathna.
×

2019/20 - Rtr. Pasan Ganewatte

Highlights

  • Year theme: Humanity Beyond Infinity
  • For the first time in RACIIT History a project (HDS) had more than 30 sponsors.
  • IGF became the 3rd Biggest E-Sports event in Sri Lanka.
  • HDS Project Chairs: Rtr. Dulitha Iddawela & Rtr. Sinthurjan Thuraiananthan
  • Sally’s Manor reached 850 participants and won the bronze award for themost outstanding Finance project at the 30th District Assembly.
  • Yet again Project E-Mithudama was nominated as the Best Community Serviceproject in South Asia.
  • In this year after HDS, all the projects were conducted through online because of the pandemic situation that the country faced.
  • RACIIT became a Club which maintained a good standard throughout the yearand the participation for the other Club events was a massive highlight during this year.
  • At the end of this year RACIIT had a member base of 354 members which was the highest the club ever had.
  • This was yet another successful year under Rtr. Pasan Ganewatte’s Presidency and became the Most Outstanding Club 3rd consecutive year in a row.

Awards

  • Most outstanding Club: Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Club Participation: Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Digital Communications Effort: Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Public Relations effort: HotDog Showdown 2020 Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Community Service Project on basic education and literacy: E-Mithudama Gold Award.
  • Most outstanding Initiative with an Interact Club: Trailblazers Silver Award.
  • President of the month for the month of August: Gold Award.
  • Most Sustainable Project of the year: E-Mithudama Bronze Award.
  • Most supportive Rotaract Club: Bronze Award.
  • Most outstanding International Service Project: Dansala Silver Award.
  • Most Outstanding President of the Year: Rtr. Pasan Ganewatte.
